## Executive Summary

This analysis provides **positional percentiles** for the Aces' top 8 rotation players, calculated against **150 WNBA players with 10+ MPG** in the 2025 season. Percentiles enable contextual comparison of each player against peers at their position.

### Key Findings:
1. **A'ja Wilson** is in the **100th percentile in 5 major categories** among centers - a generational talent
2. **Jackie Young** ranks **100th percentile PER** among guards - elite two-way production
3. **Chelsea Gray** is the team's **best facilitator** (83rd pctile AST%) but has turnover issues (12th pctile TOV%)
4. **Kierstan Bell & Aaliyah Nye** have elite 3PT attempt rates but poor efficiency - developmental priorities

## Technical Notes

### Percentile Calculation Method
- Used pandas `rank(pct=True)` for continuous ranking
- For "lower is better" stats (TOV%, DRtg, PF%), inverted percentile (1 - rank)
- Positional percentiles calculated within exact position groups (G, F, C, G-F, C-F)

### Ranking Recalculation
Original dataset had rankings based on all 184 players. New rankings (`*_rank_filtered`) are based only on 150 players with 10+ MPG, providing more meaningful context for rotation players.
