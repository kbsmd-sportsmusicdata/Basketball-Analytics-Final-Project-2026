TIER 1: COMPOSITE RATINGS (Portfolio Headlines)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. STAR RATING (0-100)
   - Formula: 40% impact + 30% efficiency + 20% usage + 10% availability
   - Business value: Single number to rank players
   - Use case: Contract negotiations, trade value, lineup optimization
   - Top 3: A'ja Wilson (96.0), Jackie Young (84.6), Chelsea Gray (62.2)

2. TWO-WAY RATING
   - Formula: OffRtg - DefRtg
   - Business value: Identifies complete players (offense + defense)
   - Use case: Starter evaluation, all-star selection
   - Top 3: NaLyssa Smith (11.3), A'ja Wilson (11.1), Jackie Young (7.9)

3. EFFICIENCY SCORE
   - Formula: TS% × USG% × 100
   - Business value: Rewards high-volume efficient scorers
   - Use case: Prop betting (over/under scoring), usage allocation
   - Top 3: A'ja Wilson (18.3), Jackie Young (14.0), Chelsea Gray (9.6)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TIER 2: OFFENSIVE METRICS (Scoring & Playmaking)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4. OFFENSIVE LOAD
   - Formula: USG% + (AST% × 0.5)
   - Business value: Total offensive responsibility
   - Use case: Fatigue management, load balancing, injury risk
   - Leaders: A'ja Wilson (0.4), Jackie Young (0.4)

5. OFFENSIVE IMPACT
   - Formula: ortg_diff (team ORtg when ON vs OFF)
   - Business value: How much player boosts team scoring
   - Use case: Lineup optimization, clutch situations
   - Leaders: A'ja Wilson (+19.6), Chelsea Gray (+19.6), Jackie Young (+14.3)

6. TRUE USAGE
   - Formula: USG% + (AST% × 0.33)
   - Business value: Captures scoring AND playmaking touches
   - Use case: More accurate usage than USG% alone
   - Leaders: A'ja Wilson, Jackie Young, Chelsea Gray

7. PLAYMAKING EFFICIENCY
   - Formula: AST% / (AST% + TOV_Ratio)
   - Business value: Clean playmaking rate (assists vs turnovers)
   - Use case: Point guard evaluation, ball-handling trust
   - Leaders: Guards with high AST%, low TOV%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TIER 3: DEFENSIVE & REBOUNDING METRICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

8. DEFENSIVE IMPACT
   - Formula: -drtg_diff (flipped so positive = good)
   - Business value: How much player improves team defense
   - Use case: Defensive stopper identification, matchup planning
   - Leaders: A'ja Wilson (+9.8), Kierstan Bell (+8.5), Jackie Young (+8.2)

9. REBOUNDING IMPACT
   - Formula: REB% × mpg / 10
   - Business value: Total rebounding contribution (rate × volume)
   - Use case: Second-chance point prevention/creation
   - Leaders: Bigs with high REB% and high minutes

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TIER 4: ROLE & USAGE METRICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

10. IMPACT PER 36
    - Formula: net_rtg_diff × (36 / mpg)
    - Business value: Normalized impact metric (per-36 standard)
    - Use case: Fair comparison across different minute allocations
    - Leaders: A'ja Wilson (32.6), Jackie Young (25.8)

11. MINUTES LEVERAGE
    - Formula: mpg / team_avg_mpg (30 min baseline)
    - Business value: How much team relies on player
    - Use case: Rotation planning, rest management
    - Leaders: Starters with 30+ mpg

12. ROLE CLARITY
    - Formula: max(USG%, AST%, REB%) / sum(all three)
    - Business value: How specialized vs versatile player is
    - Use case: Roster construction, lineup fit
    - High = Specialists, Low = Swiss Army Knife players

13. VERSATILITY SCORE
    - Formula: 1 - role_clarity
    - Business value: How well-rounded the player is
    - Use case: Small-ball lineups, positionless basketball
    - Leaders: Players balanced across scoring/passing/rebounding

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TIER 5: TEAM CONTRIBUTION METRICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

14. WIN CONTRIBUTION
    - Formula: (mpg / 48) × win_pct × 100
    - Business value: Minutes-weighted winning impact
    - Use case: Contract year performance, MVP candidacy
    - Leaders: High-minute players on winning teams

15. AVAILABILITY
    - Formula: games_count / max_games
    - Business value: Durability percentage
    - Use case: Contract reliability, injury history evaluation
    - Range: 0.696 to 1.0 (39-56 games of 56 possible)
